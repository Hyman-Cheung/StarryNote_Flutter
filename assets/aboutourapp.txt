About Our App
App Name: Starry Note

Overview:
Starry Note is a powerful note-taking application designed to help you capture, organize, and manage your thoughts and ideas effortlessly. Whether you're a student or professional in between, our app provides the perfect platform to boost your productivity.

Key Features:
Intuitive Interface: A clean and user-friendly design that makes note-taking a breeze.
Rich Text Editing: Format your notes with different fonts, colors, and styles to enhance readability.
Organization Tools: Use folders, tags, and categories to keep your notes organized and easy to find.
Search Functionality: Quickly find what you need with our powerful search feature.

Our Mission:
At Starry Note, we believe that everyone has ideas worth sharing. Our mission is to empower users with the tools they need to capture and develop their thoughts, fostering creativity and productivity.
